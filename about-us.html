about sustainathon

Diversified teams of students, ideally consisting of about five individuals with differentiated strengths and skills, will identify a real-world problem in the Indian community or society that is relevant to any of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). They will then propose a viable solution while registering for the project. The students will further shape their proposal with the support of mentors assigned to them.

There are two Phases:
1. The Ideation Phase: Developed in the period leading up to the Sustainathon on 11th April 2023.
2. The Implementation Phase: Executed in the year following the Sustainathon. (Interested and qualified students will get opportunity to continue)

Guidelines for Participants

The guidelines for Idea Presentation in the Sustainathon 2023 event at Manav Rachna University. Each idea presentation should be no longer than 10 minutes.

  1. Participants should be clear and concise about their idea's objective, how it works, and its potential impact on sustainability.
  2. Visual aids such as PowerPoint presentations, charts, or images can be used to supplement the idea.
  3. Participants should explain how their idea is related to one or more of the SDGs.
  4. The idea should be innovative and have the potential to make a significant impact on sustainability and the chosen SDG.
  5. Participants should be clear and concise about their idea's alignment with the chosen SDG.
  6. Participants should also consider any potential challenges or limitations associated with their idea and how they plan to address them.
  7. After the presentation, a question-and-answer round will follow. Participants should be prepared to answer questions from the judges or other participants.
  8. Participants should be confident, enthusiastic, and engaging during their presentation.
  9. The presentation should be informative and innovative to attract the judges' attention.
  10. The presentation should highlight the potential impact of the idea on sustainability and the chosen SDG.
  11. Participants are advised to practice their presentations beforehand to avoid any last-minute glitches.

These guidelines will help the participants to prepare an effective and compelling idea presentation that showcases their ideas and highlights their potential impact on sustainability. We wish all the participants the best of luck in the Sustainathon event at Manav Rachna University.
